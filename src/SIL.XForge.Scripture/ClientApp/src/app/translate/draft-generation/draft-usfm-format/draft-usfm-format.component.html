Your draft has been translated. However, there are some formatting options that you can choose before using your draft.

Mid-verse paragraph breaks Scripture Forge translates each verse as a whole and can't always place paragraph breaks in the right place. Choose how the paragraph breaks in source verses should appear in the draft. Remove Mid-verse paragraph breaks in the source will be omitted in the draft. Best guess Mid-verse paragraph breaks in the source will be placed at the best guess of where they should be in the draft. Move to end Mid-verse paragraph breaks in the source will be moved to the end of the verse in the draft. Quote style [coming soon] Scripture Forge can't always preserve quotes from from the source text. Choose how quotes should appear in the draft. Straight quotes: " " Use only straight quotes in the draft. Curly quotes: “ ” Attempt to use curly quotes, even though they may not always be correct.
